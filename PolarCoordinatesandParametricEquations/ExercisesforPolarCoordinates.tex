\begin{question}
In Exercises \ref{polarpointgraphfirst} - \ref{polarpointgraphlast}, plot the point given in polar coordinates in the $xy$-plane and then give three different expressions for the point such that  (a) $r < 0$ and $0 \leq \theta \leq 2\pi$,  (b) $r > 0$ and $\theta \leq 0$ (c) $r > 0$ and $\theta \geq 2\pi$
